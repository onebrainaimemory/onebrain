Connect URL

Easiest

The simplest way to connect any AI to your brain. One URL, paste it into any AI tool's custom instructions — done.

https://api.onebrain.rocks/v1/connect/ob_a1b2c3d4_your_secret_key

How to use it:
1. Copy the URL above
2. Open your AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) → Settings / Custom Instructions
3. Paste the URL — the AI will automatically fetch your brain context
